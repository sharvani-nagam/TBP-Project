Lifestyle Improvements

Follow the 20-20-20 rule

Every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds

Reduce screen time

Limit continuous screen use to prevent digital eye strain

Proper lighting

Avoid glare and ensure adequate lighting when reading or working

Wear sunglasses

Protect eyes from UV rays with 100% UV protection sunglasses

Stay hydrated

Drink 8-10 glasses of water daily to prevent dry eyes

Get adequate sleep

7-8 hours of sleep helps eyes recover and stay healthy

Eye Exercises

Perform these exercises daily to reduce eye strain and improve focus:

1. Focus Change

Hold finger 10 inches away, focus on it, then focus on something 20 feet away. Repeat 10 times.

2. Figure Eight

Imagine a large 8 on the floor 10 feet away. Trace it with your eyes slowly for 30 seconds each direction.

3. Palming

Rub hands together, place warm palms over closed eyes. Relax for 3 minutes in darkness.

4. Near and Far Focus

Hold thumb 10 inches away, focus for 15 seconds, then focus on something 20 feet away for 15 seconds. Repeat 5 times.

5. Eye Rolling

Slowly roll eyes in clockwise direction 10 times, then counter-clockwise 10 times.
